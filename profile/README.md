# Tongji TOPS Research Group

Research Team for **Traffic OPerations and Simulation (TOPS)**, Tongji University.  

---

## Research Focus
- **Traffic Operations & Simulation**: multi-scale traffic modeling, calibration, evaluation
- **Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS)**: data-driven prediction, optimization, control
- **Automated Driving Safety & Human Factors**: behavior modeling, interaction, risk assessment
- **Benchmarks & Tooling**: datasets, reproducibility, evaluation pipelines

---
